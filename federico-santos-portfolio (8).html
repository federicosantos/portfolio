How I Work

High ROAS isn't luck. It's architecture.

Results don't come from running ads. They come from the system behind them. Every engagement follows the same five-phase framework, whether it's a single brand or a 16-account portfolio.

01
Audit
I start by diagnosing what's actually happening: tracking integrity, campaign structure, bidding logic, audience overlap and budget waste. Most accounts have 20 to 40% of spend going nowhere before any optimization starts.
02
Restructure
I rebuild campaign architecture around business goals, not platform defaults. That means proper funnel segmentation (prospecting to retargeting to retention), match type hygiene and feed optimization for Shopping/PMax.
03
Track
Clean data is non-negotiable. I implement or audit conversion tracking via GA4 + GTM, set up custom events and validate attribution before making any budget decisions. You can't optimize what you can't measure.
04
Test & Scale
Systematic A/B testing across creatives, audiences and bid strategies, with clear hypotheses and statistical thresholds. When something works, I scale it fast. Budget gets redistributed weekly based on performance signals, not gut feeling.
05
Report
Every client gets a Looker Studio dashboard with the metrics that matter to their business, not vanity numbers. Monthly executive reports translate performance data into strategic decisions: where to push, where to pull, what's next.
